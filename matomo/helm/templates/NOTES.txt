To connect your WordPress site to Matomo:

1. Install "Connect Matomo" plugin on WordPress:
   https://wordpress.org/plugins/wp-piwik/

2. Configure plugin with:
   - Matomo URL: https://{{ .Values.global.domain }}
   - Auth Token: Generate in Matomo → Settings → Personal → Security

3. Enable tracking and view analytics!
